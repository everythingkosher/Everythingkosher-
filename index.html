Our Story

At Everything Kosher, our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: bringing premium kosher comfort food to everyone — wherever they are.

What started as a humble food truck in the heart of the Woodbury Common Premium Outlets has grown into a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike.

We believe great food has the power to bring people together. That’s why we’re committed to serving delicious, freshly made dishes using only the finest ingredients — all under the highest kosher standards. Whether you’re craving a quick bite on-the-go or gathering with family and friends, we’re here to make every meal memorable.

From our flavorful Mediterranean fusion menu to our warm, welcoming atmosphere, we’re more than just a food truck — we’re a community built on taste, quality, and care.

Taste and quality are our top priority — and your smiles and stories inspire us to keep raising the bar every single day.

Come visit us, share a meal, and become part of the Everything Kosher family.

We can’t wait to serve you!
